# Return to the hive
To return to the hive, you must take advantage of the free transportation offered on Saturdays during the VS.

{% include image.html url="/assets/img/screens/tuto_tp_vs.webp" description="Utilisation du TP du VS" %}

Once that day has passed, you must purchase an advanced TP in the alliance store to return to the hive.
