## But

Get all 18 reward chests every day.

## Swap time slots

Two slots can be swapped once a day. If the chests of the current slot have already been opened, the rewards are kept. The new slot resets the rewards and becomes the current slot until the next scheduled change. 

{% include translated_image.html url="events/caa/caa_change.webp" description="Swap CAA time slots" %}

# Time slots

## Hero Advancement

{% include translated_image.html url="events/caa/caa_heros.webp" description="Hero advancement slot" %}

To get the 12,000 points needed to get the three chests, you need:

| Action             | Quantité requise                                                                                   |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1 Hero recuitement | 30 tickets (12000 / 400 = 30)                                                                      |
| Use 2000 hero EXP  | 24M hero EXP (12000 * 2000 = 24M)<br/>To upgrade a lvl 93 hero (to Lvl 94), it needs 24M hero exp. |

## Base building

{% include image.html url="/assets/img/caa/caa_batiments.webp" description="Base building slot" %}

To get the 24,000 points needed to get the three chests, you need:

| Action                           | Quantité requise                            |
|----------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Increase Building Power by 1 pt  | 24000 points                                |
| Accelerate buildings by 1 minute | 2400 minutes = 40 hours (24000 / 10 = 2400) |


## Unit progression

{% include image.html url="/assets/img/caa/caa_unites.webp" description="Unit progression slot" %}

To get the 60,000 points needed to get the three chests, you need:

| Action                           | Quantité requise                                   |
|----------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| Train 1 Level 7 unit             | 2728 level 7 units to train (60000 / 22 = 2727,27) |
| Accelerate buildings by 1 minute | 6000 minutes = 100 hours (60000 / 10 = 6000)       |

## Tech research

{% include image.html url="/assets/img/caa/caa_techno.webp" description="Tech research slot" %}

To get the 24,000 points needed to get the three chests, you need:

| Action                        | Quantité requise                            |
|-------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Increase tech power by 1 pt   | 24000 points                                |
| Accelerate search by 1 minute | 2400 minutes = 40 hours (24000 / 10 = 2400) |


## Drone Boost

{% include image.html url="/assets/img/caa/caa_drone.webp" description="Drone Boost slot" %}

To get the 30,000 points needed to get the three chests, you need:

| Action                             | Quantité requise                     |
|------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Use 10 points of Drone Combat Data | 300 000 points (10 * 30000 = 300000) |
| Use 1 Stamina                      | 300 Endurance (30000 / 100 = 300)    |
