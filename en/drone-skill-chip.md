# Puces de compétence de drone

<a href="https://youtu.be/cBpZLbk4rBc" target="_blank">Vidéo explicative</a>

At **day 82**, players unlock the Combat Boost and Drone Skill Chip systems.

**Skill Chips** are very useful for increasing your squad's defense, HP, and attack. The higher the
rarity level, the greater the bonus.

## Laboratoire de puces

Quickly upgrade the building to **level 20** to unlock UR Skill Chip crafting.

Quickly obtain a **full set of UR Skill Chips**.

Use SSRs to level up Combat Boost.
