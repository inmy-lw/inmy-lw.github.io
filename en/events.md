# Different recurring events

## Train

It is possible to take the train every day. Each carriage has different types of resources. You can choose the carriage you want to travel in.

{% include translated_image.html url="events/train/train.webp" description="Alliance train" %}

You need to hand in trade tickets (the same ones used to send UR trucks).

{% include translated_image.html url="events/train/tickets.webp" description="Using trade tickets (Please do not click 'Like and thank' ğŸ˜‰)" %}

A carriage has 5 seats. If more than 5 people try to enter the same carriage, the passengers are chosen at random.

Once the tickets are handed in, it is possible to change carriage (by selecting a new one) until departure.
