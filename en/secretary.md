## Accessing different positions

You can access them from your profile picture, then by clicking on your server name.

{% include translated_image.html url="secretary.webp" description="Accessing different positions" %}

## Secretary of Strategy

Start healing at the moment you are appointed to this position.

| Boost               | Value |
|---------------------|-------|
| Hospital's Capacity | +20 % |
| Unit Healing Speed  | +20 % |

## Secretary of Security

Start trainings/promotions at the moment you are appointed to this position.

| Boost                   | Value |
|-------------------------|-------|
| Unit Training Cap       | +20 % |
| Increase Training Speed | +20 % |

## Secretary of Development

Start constructions/research at the moment you are appointed to this position.

| Boost              | Value |
|--------------------|-------|
| Construction Speed | +50 % |
| Research Speed     | +25 % |

_A calculator for construction/research durations is available [on this page](/{{page.lang}}/tools.html)._

## Secretary of Science

Start constructions/research at the moment you are appointed to this position.

| Boost              | Value |
|--------------------|-------|
| Research Speed     | +50 % |
| Construction Speed | +25 % |

_A calculator for construction/research durations is available [on this page](/{{page.lang}}/tools.html)._

## Secretary of the Interior

Collect (by shaking the phone or tapping the relevant resources) after being appointed to this position.

| Boost             | Value  |
|-------------------|--------|
| Boost Food Output | +100 % |
| Boost Iron Output | +100 % |
| Boost Gold Output | +100 % |

{% include image.html url="/assets/img/screens/resource_gathering.webp" description="Resource collection" %}

_This position does not increase Armored Truck resources__

## Vice President

This position can only be assigned by the president.

| Boost                   | Value |
|-------------------------|-------|
| Construction Speed      | +20 % |
| Research Speed          | +20 % |
| Increase Training Speed | +10 % |
